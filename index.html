Wyoming

I was listening to this cover when my mom almost got attacked by a bear in Wyoming. Her voice is so somber and it's really sad how the song describes her in a way, but somehow the song is also comforting.

I was clearly a bit obsessed with covers during my trip to Wyoming (I also was listening to Taylor's cover of Riptide religiously). This cover never fails to bring a smile to my face. My mom is definetly sick of me blasting it, but I don't care.

France

Before I start let me just say that French radio is quite unique. The music is always a few years old and they play some special songs. One year they would constantly play this song and since I was probably six or something like that my parents would always switch the channel, but we'd still hear the wiggle wiggle wiggle at the beginning hehe.

This is another song they would constantly play on the radio. My mom joked one day that it would be a great song to wake up my sister with, so ever since then I have woken her up by storming into her room, opening up the blinds, and blasting this song. On a seperate note, I do not understand why people would voluntarily sleep with their blinds open. I mean I get it if you have neighbors close by or you are on the first floor, but why else would you do it?

I think I have told you about this, but I have some very distinct memories of playing Lover, the album, in France. I remember listening to this while sitting on a stone wall that was on the edge of a cliff after dinner. That was probably the worst day of my life, bit in that moment everything felt okay.

This story is similar, but it was the day Lover was released. I was playing this for my family in the car while we were driving back from dinner.

This was the right before Lover was released and I was playing this song while walking home from dinner. WHY ARE ALL THESE MEMORIES FROM RIGHT AFTER DINNER?!?!
